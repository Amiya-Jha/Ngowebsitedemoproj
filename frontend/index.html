Our Goals

Children Foundation works for the social upliftment of the backward section of the society. Our mission is to provide needy children with all the basic necessities they deserve.

Children are the future and we solely aim for their development and build a strong foundation which will help them to achieve great peaks and suceess in their careers. Children Foundation has given wings to girl students as well so that they can fly in their careers with beautiful colours.
